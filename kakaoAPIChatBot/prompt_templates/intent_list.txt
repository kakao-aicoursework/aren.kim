social: The KakaoTalk Social API provides a user's KakaoTalk profile and friend information for implementing social features in your service. You can also send KakaoTalk messages based on the provided KakaoTalk friend information.
sync: KakaoSync is a business solution that helps you sign up for services more conveniently through Kakao Login, a social login.
talkchannel: It is a service that allows you to spread various service news to KakaoTalk users in the form of messages and posts. The KakaoTalk channel provides the ability to send marketing messages to users who are your friends. A friend is a user who has added KakaoTalk Channel as a friend. To reduce costs, you can create friend groups based on information such as user gender, age, region, and rating, and send messages only to users who are expected to be more effective.
